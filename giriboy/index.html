About

Hong Si-Young (Korean: 홍시영; born January 24, 1991), well known by his stage name, Giriboy (Hangul: 기리보이), is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer signed to Just Music. Giriboy debuted in 2011 with the song “You look so good to me” and participated in TV show “Show Me The Money 3”, aired in 2014, managing to survive until the first round of finals, where he was eliminated against the rapper C Jamm. In 2018 he was one of the judges in “Show Me The Money 777” in the team Just Music together with swings. he did the same the following year in “Show Me The Money 8”, where he was part of BGM-v crew alone with Verbal Jint, Bewhy and Millic. Giriboy was also one of the mentors in season 1 (2017) and season 3 (2019) of “High School Rapper” TV show. His signature on his songs is “G.R” or “G.R.BOY” at the beginning of his songs or of the songs that he produces. He has a lot of tattoos on his arms and the number “148” tattooed on his neck (he said that this was a number that he used to see often in his dreams, and it is also is favourite number). he also has a dog.
